## Interpretation notes

1. PM metrics are treated as weak labels produced by Emotiv, not as expert ground truth.
2. PM columns are excluded from X to avoid direct target leakage.
3. The main comparison criterion is GroupKFold by subject_id.
4. Spearman is important because it measures whether the model preserves the ordering of cognitive state intensity.
5. Targets with higher GroupKFold Spearman/R2 are better candidates for the main project target.
