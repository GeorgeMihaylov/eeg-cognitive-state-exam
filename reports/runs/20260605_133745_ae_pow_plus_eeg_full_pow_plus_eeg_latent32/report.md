## Interpretation

This experiment adds a generative deep learning block to the project. The autoencoder is trained to reconstruct EEG/POW feature vectors. Synthetic vectors are generated by adding Gaussian noise in the latent space and decoding the perturbed latent representations. This is not raw EEG waveform generation; it is generation in the prepared EEG/POW feature space. The approach is used as a practical lightweight approximation for the exam project.
